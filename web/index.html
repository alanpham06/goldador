Goldador

Membership & access for the Labrador committee (ScottyLabs)

What this is

Goldador is the home for how the Labrador committee tracks who belongs to the group and what they are allowed to use—things like GitHub org membership, shared drives, and team channels. Rather than juggling one-off emails or informal lists, Labrador keeps that information organized in one trusted place so access can stay clear and consistent.

Why it matters

When onboarding is predictable, people get the tools they need sooner, and the committee can be confident permissions match who is officially on record. Automated checks behind the scenes help catch mistakes early so registrations stay tidy.

How to register

If you want to join or update your Labrador access, step-by-step instructions—including prerequisites, what to submit, and how approvals work—are in the project wiki.
